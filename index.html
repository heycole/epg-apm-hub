Move-Out Checklist

  • Send move-out notice to tenant
  • Confirm final rent payment received
  • Schedule property walk-through
  • Document property condition with photos
  • Collect keys and access devices
  • Process security deposit disposition
  • Send itemized deduction list if applicable
  • Return security deposit (Enterprise Bank)

Maintenance Request Process

  • Receive and log maintenance request in Buildium
  • Assess priority and urgency level
  • Contact EZ Home Repair (888-906-7626) for quote
  • If over $500: Obtain 3 bids and owner approval
  • Schedule work with approved vendor
  • Coordinate access with tenant
  • Verify work completion and invoice accuracy
  • Record completion in Buildium and process payment

Monthly Owner Report

  • Gather all rent payments and fees collected
  • Reconcile ledger with bank deposits
  • Compile maintenance and repair expenses
  • Include tenant contact information and status
  • Calculate owner distribution (if applicable)
  • Review report for accuracy and completeness
  • Send to owner via email or portal
  • Archive for record-keeping
